Term and Conditions

No artist may place a bid on their own work. In the event that a bidder wins the auction and the artwork is in perfect condition, the artwork must be purchased.

If the winning bidder want to conduct an auction, they may upload an image of the artwork but they must identify the creator. They are unable to claim the artist's work.

Type 1: At the first stage, a pre-qualification request is filed, and at the second stage, a package containing a tender proposal (comprising technical and commercial aspects) is submitted. The second step will invite the bidders (based on the results of the pre-qualification evaluation).

Type 2: A technical and pre-qualification request are filed in the first stage, followed by the commercial portion of the bid in the second. The bidders will receive invitations to the next round (based on the pre-qualification and bid technical part evaluation).

Documents that serve as the basis for bids and contain all the information a potential bidder needs to create a bid for the goods, infrastructure projects, and consulting services that will be offered are referred to as bidding documents.
